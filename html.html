Our Story

The R.C. Ramos Story

Founded in 1995 by Engr. Rodrigo C. Ramos, R.C. Ramos Construction began as a small family business with big dreams. What started as a modest operation with just a handful of employees has grown into one of the most respected construction firms in the Philippines.

Our journey has been marked by steady growth, strategic expansion, and an unwavering commitment to quality. From our first residential project to the large-scale commercial developments we undertake today, every milestone has been built on a foundation of integrity and craftsmanship.

Through economic challenges and industry changes, we've remained true to our core values while continuously innovating to meet the evolving needs of our clients and the construction industry.

Our Journey Through Time

Key milestones in our company's history and growth

1995

Humble Beginnings

R.C. Ramos Construction was established as a small general contracting firm in Metro Manila, taking on residential and small commercial projects.

2002

First Major Project

Completed our first large-scale commercial project, the 10-story Ramos Tower in Makati, marking our entry into high-rise construction.

2008

Triple "A" Accreditation

Awarded prestigious PCAB accreditation, recognizing our capabilities to undertake major construction projects nationwide.

2015

Regional Expansion

Opened our Cebu regional office to serve growing demand in the Visayas region, completing our first project outside Luzon.

2023

Green Construction

Launched our sustainable building division to meet growing demand for eco-friendly, energy-efficient construction solutions.
