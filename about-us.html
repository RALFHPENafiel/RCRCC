Our Story

Founded in 1995, R.C. Ramos Construction Corporation has grown from a local contractor in Pampanga to a nationally recognized Triple "A" construction firm in the Philippines.

For over two decades, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting high-quality infrastructure and delivering end-to-end construction solutions that support national development.

Our journey has been built on a foundation of integrity, exceptional craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success. From humble beginnings to becoming one of the most trusted names in Philippine construction, we continue to push boundaries while staying true to our core values.
